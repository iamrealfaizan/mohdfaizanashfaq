# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Primary users are recruiters and clients evaluating Mohammed Faizan Ashfaq for AI engineering, product engineering, and technical leadership work. They arrive with limited time and need fast proof of credibility, shipped outcomes, and communication clarity.

## Product Purpose

This portfolio exists to convert attention into trust. It should present Mohammed as an AI engineer who ships production-grade LLM systems, RAG pipelines, and AI-powered workflows, while also making it easy for recruiters and clients to contact him or review his resume.

## Brand Personality

Bold, credible, visionary. The voice should feel direct and high-conviction, with strong claims grounded in real delivery outcomes rather than hype.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS portfolio layouts, soft rounded cards, purple-gradient AI branding, dark mode drift, and oversized undifferentiated tech-stack dumps. Do not let the site read like a template, a Dribbble clone, or a vague “future of AI” landing page with no proof.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with proof, not abstraction.
2. Use typography and layout to create authority before detail.
3. Keep every section useful to both hiring and buying decisions.
4. Let the visual language feel editorial-tech, but keep the message practical.
5. Build the homepage as a strong landing page now and a scalable portfolio shell later.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and keyboard accessibility for interactive controls. Motion should remain purposeful and degrade cleanly under reduced-motion preferences. Information should remain scannable on mobile, tablet, and desktop without relying on hover or animation for comprehension.
